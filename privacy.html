VeganEye Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 24, 2025

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Profile information

App Usage Data

  • Scan history (products you've scanned)
  • Favorite products
  • Community product submissions
  • App language preference
  • Theme preference (light/dark)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide barcode scanning services
  • To save your scan history for future reference
  • To authenticate your account
  • To remember your app preferences
  • To allow community product contributions

Data Storage

  • Cloud data is stored securely using Supabase (EU servers)
  • Local preferences stored on your device
  • All data encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • We comply with GDPR regulations

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Track you across other apps

We only share data with:

  • Supabase (our secure database provider)
  • Open Food Facts API (product info - no personal data shared)

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access all your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a readable format
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of any analytics

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all data directly in the app settings, or contact us for assistance.

Children's Privacy

VeganEye is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests:
Email: privacy@veganeye.app

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users via the app of any material changes to this privacy policy.
